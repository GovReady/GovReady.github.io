Help us build and open source expert system for FISMA

The Department of Homeland Security Science and Technology Directorate awarded $1.1 million to GovReady PBC (Public Benefit Corp) to provide tools that improve accessibility for small to mid-size companies interested in developing software and IT systems for the federal market. Our mission is to create open source tools, including an expert system, to help agile software developers get new software and apps through the complex, time-consuming FISMA Assessment and Authorization process.
