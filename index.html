About

Open does not mean too insecure for government use.

Real risks and costs come from time-consuming security certification processes that delay deployments and frustrate innovation.

GovReady aims to speed and standarize IT certificaton the way Bootstrap speeds and standardizes front-end web site design.

Download the GovReady pre-release and help develop a better way to secure and certify IT.

Part of the GitMachines project to support government innovation with tools and servers that meet government regulations so developers can easily build and adopt new technology. Winner of John S. and James L. Knight Foundation 2013 News Challenge. Learn more...
